CONTROLS

W: move forward
Z: move forward
S: move backward
A: strafe left
Q: strafe left
D: strafe right
LEFT ARROW: turn left
RIGHT ARROW: turn right
UP ARROW: look up
DOWN ARROW: look down
C: crouch
P: pause
R: reload
RETURN: activate
SHIFT: run
SPACE: attack
M: next weapon
L: previous weapon
ESC: quit game
MOUSE LEFT BUTTON: shoot
MOUSE MIDDLE BUTTON: crouch
MOUSE RIGHT BUTTON: reload
MOUSE WHEEL UP: next weapon
MOUSE WHEEL DOWN: previous weapon
NUMERICAL PAD 8: move forward
NUMERICAL PAD 2: move backward
NUMERICAL PAD 4: strafe left
NUMERICAL PAD 6: strafe right
F11: switch to windowed or fullscreen mode
