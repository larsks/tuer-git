TUER : Truly Ununsual Experience of Revolution
alpha version (quite stable, with a few bugs)

Single player first person shooter 
created by Julien Gouesse (engineer in computer science, alumni of the University Pierre and Marie Curie Paris 6 Jussieu)
largely inspired from "Art Attack" by Vincent Stahl (www.stahlforce.com)

project under GPL licence (version 2)

requires OpenGL 1.3 and Java Runtime Environment 1.7

please report any bug: http://gouessej.wordpress.com
