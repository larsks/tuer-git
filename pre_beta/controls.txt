CONTROLS

W: move forward                      
Z: move forward                      
S: move backward                     
A: strafe left                       
Q: strafe left                       
D: strafe right                      
LEFT ARROW: turn left                
RIGHT ARROW: turn right              
UP ARROW: look up                    
DOWN ARROW: look down                
C: crouch                            
P: pause
R: reload
RETURN: activate
SHIFT: run
SPACE: attack
M: next weapon
L: previous weapon
ESC: quit game
MOUSE LEFT BUTTON: shoot
MOUSE MIDDLE BUTTON: crouch
MOUSE RIGHT BUTTON: reload
MOUSE WHEEL UP: next weapon
MOUSE WHEEL DOWN: previous weapon
